Design a modern mobile app called Virtual TaskMaster.

The app connects users with live human operators wearing 360° cameras who stream their real-world view to the user. The user can watch and guide the operator in real time.

Key concept:
Users can switch between First-Person View (FPS) (seeing exactly what the operator sees) and Third-Person View (TPS) (seeing the operator and surroundings).

The app should feel futuristic, immersive, and simple to use.
